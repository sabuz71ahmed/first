11.5.3 On GitHub
Make sure you’re signed into GitHub. Click on your profile pic in upper 
right corner and go Settings, then SSH and GPG keys. Click “New SSH key”
. Paste your public key in the “Key” box. Give it an informative title,
presumably related to the comment you used above, during key creation. 
For example, you might use 2018-mbp to record the year and computer. Click “Add SSH key”
